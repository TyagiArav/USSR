Environment and Conservation

Protecting the environment is our top priority. We will promote sustainable practices and renewable energy sources to ensure a healthy planet for future generations.

Inflation and Economy

We believe that everyone should have access to a fair and stable economy. We will work to reduce inflation and provide economic opportunities for all students.

Gun Violence

We will work to end gun violence on campus by implementing common-sense gun laws that protect our students and community.

People’s Rights and Full Equality

We believe in equal rights for all students regardless of race, gender, or sexual orientation. We will fight to ensure that all students are treated with dignity and respect.

Frat Parties

We understand that students want to have fun, but we also believe in responsible partying. We will work with fraternities to ensure that parties are safe and respectful to all students.
